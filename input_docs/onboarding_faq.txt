Onboarding FAQ

What equipment will I receive?
New employees receive a company laptop, charger, and any role-specific peripherals.

Who do I contact for IT support?
IT support can be reached through the internal helpdesk portal or by emailing the IT support team.

How do I request system access?
System access requests must be submitted through the access management system and approved by your manager.

What training is mandatory?
All employees must complete security awareness training within the first 30 days of employment.
