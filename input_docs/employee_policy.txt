Employee Policy Document

All employees must comply with company security and operational policies.

Password Policy:
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Passwords must be rotated every 90 days. Password reuse is not allowed for the last 5 passwords.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees are permitted to work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Remote work requires a secure internet connection and company-approved devices.

Access Control:
Employees must not share login credentials under any circumstances. Access to internal systems is granted based on role and reviewed quarterly.

Violations:
Failure to comply with company policies may result in disciplinary action, including termination.
